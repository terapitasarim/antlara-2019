Dental Implant FAQ

Implant Treatments - Antalya

Dental implant application is mostly done in two stages. In the first stage, the tooth is removed by local anaesthesia and the gum ablation is performed. The bone thickness and length of the bone are taken into consideration for the implant. In the second stage, dental screwing is performed on the root of the tooth.

The first stage of implantation is the process of performing the necessary examination and inserting the implant screw into the bone. After this process, the duration of the prosthesis may vary between 2-6 months depending on the age of the person, the quality of the jaw bone. You will be notified of this period after the examination by the dentist.

Implant application is performed in two stages. If local anaesthesia is applied correctly in the first stage, there is no pain during the application. When the implant is placed in the jawbone, a slight sore may be heard in the evening. This mild pain is easily suppressed by painkillers. The pain is not very different from the pain in normal tooth extraction. Over time, the mouth will be used to the implant will not feel even in your mouth.

Diabetes and Diabetes Patients;
Patients with diabetes certainly cannot say that the implant will not be applied. The level of the disease must be under control in patients with diabetes. Diabetes patients are considered to be at risk because of the slow tissue healing and the high risk of infection. Implantable diabetic patients should be careful not to interfere with the dentist's controls after the treatment and to pay attention to the hygiene rules for oral care after treatment. Because patients with high blood pressure are more responsive to conditions such as pain or stress, blood pressure may increase during implant treatment and cause problems. For this reason, blood pressure of people with blood pressure during implant application should be followed.
In Bone Development;
Bone development of human beings is not complete at an early age. Implant application to be done as a result of completion of the jaw bone to hold the implant can be a problem in the bone. Therefore, it is expected that the appropriate time for implanting in such patients.
Conditions of Jaw Bone Unfavourable;
In implant applications, the amount of jaw bone to which the implant will be placed is very important. The titanium implants used for the implant are usually produced in a certain diameter and length. According to the condition of the jaw bone dentist decides the size of the implant. In some cases, the person's jawbone may be too thin or not long enough to allow implants. In such cases, a decision can be made that the dentist can adjust the jawbone by surgical intervention with the patient. Methods for this are briefly referred to as bone grafting, bone opening. In addition to these conditions, patients with heart disease or cardiac pacemakers, in patients undergoing chemotherapy can be done by the dentist and dental examination by the dentist to make the patient decide together. If the implant is applied, the patients should follow the oral care and hygiene rules and the dentist controls very well after the treatment.
Those with Blood Clotting Problem;
In patients with blood clotting disorder, implantation is not usually applied because it is very risky.

The prosthesis inserted after the implant is not much different from the individual's own teeth. For this reason, gum diseases can be affected by processes such as tooth inflammation. With the necessary dentist controls and good oral care, implant prostheses can be used for a long time.

Implant applications can be applied to anyone if the person does not have diseases such as diabetes and high blood pressure. However, some conditions are required for implant application. These will be checked during the pre-treatment examination or during a doctor's interview.
One of the conditions that should be in pre-implant examinations is the measurement of the jaw bone of the person. The jawbone should be wide and deep enough to accommodate the implant screw. Otherwise, the implant screw cannot be held, and the implant application fails. It also determines the quality of the prosthesis to be made on the hardness of the jawbone.

Age is not important for implant application as long as the person's general health condition is good. However, implant application may not be preferred if the jaw bone structure of the implant application person is not completed.

Titanium is the most widely used and accepted implant material in the world. It is used by dentists because it fits quickly to the body and is not easily rejected by the body. However, after the implant, if the necessary oral care cannot be performed, there may be a risk of infection in patients as a result of non-compliance with hygiene rules. This may result in premature implant completion of the implant. For this reason, dentist checks, and oral care should not be disturbed after implantation.

What are the Benefits and Losses of Implant?

Benefits of Implant

One of the most important advantages and benefits of implants is that it is safer and more robust than other treatment methods. Another important advantage is that it does not depend on other teeth and is a structure that supports itself from the dental bone. Implant application is one of the most preferred dental treatments by dentists, since it may be the case that the teeth become eroded by the help of other solid teeth in bridge or crown treatments.

In addition, since the titanium material used in implant construction is one of the most easily adaptable materials to human tooth structure, dental implant applications are more prominent than other treatment methods. We invite you to our clinic to answer your questions about implant in Antalya.

Because of the disadvantages of moving prostheses before implant treatments, implant treatments are more preferred today. Movable prostheses were difficult to use and could have lost food residues under the prosthesis. As a result of this, both in terms of hygiene and mouth odour in order to make fixed dentures are placed on the implants are used more actively in dental treatment.

To summarize the benefits of implants;

  • Natural tooth structure
  • Easy fitting of titanium material used in construction
  • To be able to chew as a normal tooth structure and to eat what one wants
  • Contribute to healthy nutrition
  • Providing aesthetic tooth image by completing missing teeth,

Disadvantages of Implant

As with any type of treatment, there may be minor risks in implant treatment and implant applications. For detailed information, who cannot be implanted section is useful to read. A high degree of success is achieved in implant applications, unless the person has such problems.

What should be considered after implant?

What is Oral Care after Implant?

The first two days after implantation is very important. During this period, oral care should be given great importance and movements that cause pressure in the mouth should be avoided. If possible, the mouth should not be shaken or gargled in the first day. Intraoral cleaning should be done by brush or dental floss; Implant areas can be cleaned with a damp cotton.

What should be the nutrition after implantation?

It is necessary to pay attention to nutrition until the period of 2-6 months after implantation of the jaw to the jaw after implantation. Keep away from excessive hot or cold foods, very hard foods should not be eaten instead of soft and warm foods should be preferred.

What should be paid attention after the implant?

  • The bottom of the implants should be cleaned with the most accurate floss. During cleaning, care should be taken not to damage the stability of the implant.
  • Intermediate brush is an important dental cleaning tool that should be used by implant patients.
  • The first 2-3 hours after the implant should not be eaten anything. For the first 24 hours, it is necessary to avoid hot and cold foods and beverages.
  • The first 12 hours of tooth brushing, or mouthwash should not be done. After 36 hours, the mouthwash should be done twice a day for 2 weeks if possible.
  • Smoking should never be used during the first 24 hours. The implant site is susceptible to infection. If possible, it is useful to stay away from smoking until full recovery is achieved. Bleeding in your mouth for 1-2 days is normal. To stop or reduce bleeding, you can clean the implant site without applying too much pressure with a soft sterile cloth or damp cotton.
  • Take care to use the painkiller or antibiotics given by the dentist as recommended. In any case, you should contact your dentist.
  • It is normal to have swelling in the mouth and face area during the first few days. Especially for the skeleton that will occur in the eye area, ice can be treated.
  • In case of unexpected situations, you should contact the dentist and ask about your problems.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS ABOUT DENTAL IMPLANT TREATMENTS

How will I spend the treatment process?

It should be waited for 2-4 months for implant and bone fusion. If the bone is interfered, this period may be 6 months.

What are the advantages of the implant?

Because the implant is directly integrated into the bone, it can be used as your own tooth. The intact teeth around the implant are not shrunk to the untreated prosthesis.

Will the surgery be difficult?

You will not feel pain because of local anaesthesia during the procedure. Before starting the procedure, you should take the medication that your doctor says and follow the procedure after the procedure.

Will there be swelling after surgery?

The swelling on the cheek can be minimized or not swollen by the first day on the ice compress. As long as you take the medication your doctor wrote, you don't have severe pain.

Is it compatible with the human body?

Titanium is compatible with the human body. It does not cause any allergies or toxic effects.

Does smoking hurt to the implant?

Smoking increases the risk of falling and affects the healing of the implant site.

How long will I use the implant?

You can use it for many years.

Who can have implant treatment?

The implant can be done to everyone as long as they carry the criteria with the bone structure and there is no infection on the implant area.

Same day Implant

Especially in cases where the bone density is sufficient in the lack of anterior teeth, it places the implant in the same session and provides the opportunity to make teeth. Thus, the waiting period of 3-4 months after placement of the implant is eliminated.

Sinus Lift and Bone Graft Implant

Sinus Lift (Sinus Base Upgrade)

Bone volume losses occur as a result of the sinus cavities. Although these losses are seen as a natural process, they cause problems in implant treatment. Sinus lift operation has moved downward sinus cavities that have been moved to the toothless area of that region by adding bone powder implant treatment is applied. By means of this process, sufficient volume is created for the length and width of the implant which must be fully inserted into the bone.

What should be considered after the sinus lifting operation? Care should be taken to protect the wound area after sinus lifting. Food should be eaten with the other side for 24 hours. Nothing should be eaten or consumed for two hours after the procedure. Care should be taken not to apply pressure and keep the mouth open while sneezing.

Bone Powder (Bone Graft)

Bone grafts consisting of three types of human origin, animal origin and synthetic are applied in powder or block form. If bone grafts are inadequate bone at the place where the implants will be made, if the implant is to be performed in the same session with the tooth extraction, it is used for the extraction of the teeth which cause chronic inflammation and long stay in the mouth and cause the bone to dissolve if the sinus base is to be performed before the implant. Bone graft helps the region to heal faster and increase bone volume.

What is a Mini Implant?

Mini implants are smaller than standard implants. Their diameters are 2 -2,8 mm. When can mini implants be used instead of standard implants? Mini implants are much easier, simpler and faster than standard implants. Mini implants are placed in the implant site with a single implant guide drill without incision in the gingiva. Immediate attachment of implants to dentures after implantation and the retention of dentures are a great advantage for the patient. The use of mini implants in elderly patients or patients with heart, diabetes and hypertension will be more problematic.

What are the advantages of mini implants?

  • Being economical
  • It is a simple, easy and quick way to fix the gingiva without having to make incision.
  • The patient's prostheses are placed in the same session.
  • It can be applied in patients with systemic diseases and in the elderly.
  • There is no swelling, pain or bleeding after implantation.

When can I use mini implants instead of standard implants?

It can be used in patients with a thin bone. The lower incisors can be applied in the teeth or in the absence of cutting teeth next to the upper incisor teeth. It can be used if there is some closing in the drafting space by pulling the molar teeth. Mini implants can be used for retention of the total prosthesis if normal implants cannot be performed in patients with systemic disease. It can be applied easily in elderly patients. It is preferred for economic reasons.

In which cases should mini implants not be used?

It should not be used in patients with bone quality. There should be at least 10 mm in the lower jaw and at least 13 mm in the upper jaw. Mini implants should not be preferred if there is no obstacle for applying the standard implant. If mini implants are to be used for the retention of total prostheses and fixed teeth are to be applied against the area to be implanted, they should be avoided. If implants are to be applied in the upper jaw and lower jaw, then mini implants should not be used. Mini implants are thought to be around 10 years in the mouth.
