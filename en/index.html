Why Antlara Dental Clinic?

In our clinic, we carry out social responsibility studies in order to raise awareness of community oral and dental health.

For this purpose, we organize dental health screening and organize oral and dental health trainings. Antlara Oral and Dental Health Polyclinic consists of highly qualified, honest and experienced staff. As a result, we normally produce fast, long lasting, aesthetic results and bring our patients to their health.

We will continue to work with a team of dentists who follow the world-class technology, know the scientific developments with the follow-up of the congress, and offer sincere, friendly and reliable service.
